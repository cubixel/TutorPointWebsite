Our own company finance manager would assess the total expenditures at the end of each week to see if restrictions needed to be tightened or eased. Each iteration would line up in conjunction with a set wage budget based on the assigned user stories time estimates. Marketing

The CUBIXEL company vision and mission statement was utilised by the CUBIXEL marketing team to direct marketing initiatives and actions during the development process for the TutorPoint product. The vision and mission statement additionally enabled the CUBIXEL marketing team to monitor the success of marketing initiatives in regard to their conformity with the CUBIXEL vision and mission statement and their contribution to achieving marketing targets. A diagram illustrating the CUBIXEL mission statement, strategy and values can be seen below, detailing what CUBIXEL consideres important regarding our customers, colleagues, and community.

The CUBIXEL marketing team undertook extensive analysis of the TutorPoint product being developed by CUBIXEL, such analysis consisted of an overview and examination of the relative strengths, weaknesses, opportunities and threats possessed by TutorPoint. Identifying and acknowledging such aspects of the CUBIXEL company and TutorPoint enables for us to act upon our strengths, ensuring that we are always working effectively and efficiently; identifying weaknesses allows us to utilise our strengths to mitigate their effects and improve as a company; highlighting opportunities for the TutorPoint product enables CUBIXEL to capitalise on them, hence, paving the way for TutorPoint to grow as a brand and product within its market; finally, identifying threats to the TutorPoint brand and product enables us to prepare for potential problematic factors, threats cannot be controlled, however, they're effects can be mitigated.

The CUBIXEL marketing team carried substantial research into the market context in which the TutorPoint product resides, that being, private and online education. By understanding market trends in the industry associated industries, CUBIXEL was able to take advantage of opportunities as they arise and act quickly to reduce any risks to development or deployment of the product. To achieve such research, primary and secondary market research methods were utilised. Utilising secondary research methods involved seeking out existing research and data sources applicable to TutorPoint's industry. A majority of secondary sources were sourced from the internet; examples including demographics and market research compiled by similar companies and products, and reports issued by research institutions. In the case where secondary research methods produced insufficient data, primary research methods were utilised, carried out personally by the CUBIXEL marketing team; the predominant method that was used was online surveys.

Marketing Strategy and Plan

The CUBIXEL vision and mission statement was utilised in the establishment of the TutorPoint marketing plan and strategy. The product marketing strategy details the TutorPoint product target market and industry, its target demographic and persona, the marketing initiatives for the TutorPoint brand and product, and the marketing channels utilised by the CUBIXEL marketing team to achieve marketing initiatives, further detailing how such channels will be used.
