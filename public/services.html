Services

Tested Modules

CUBIXEL follows the test-driven development (TDD) methodology when it comes to software. This ensures a higher quality of code for our final product by reducing the number of bugs present in production code. The software also undergoes exploratory testing at the end of each iteration to solidify functionality.

Client Involvement

An on-site customer or representative will be assigned to our team to help in decisions relating to product features and to ensure the final deliverables are within scope. Product demos of the software's current state can also be presented to the client when requested.

Quality Assurance

Our quality assurance managers set and review the current performance to ensure the company is meeting its own standards. Our code refactoring and documentation standards are also cross checked to provide quick stable future development.

Quality Assurance

The CUBIXEL company prides itself on its ability to provide quality assurance as a means to ensure a high level of quality in production during the development of products or services.

The CUBIXEL Quality Assurance Manual provides an understanding of what the CUBIXEL company values and what it represents. It details the corporate structure of CUBIXEL and information regarding the responsibilities of each role within our development team. Our QA Manual serves as a reference for how the company's software engineering projects should run, providing details on many aspects of CUBIXEL’s particular approach to project management; we regularly update our QA Manual to reflect the dynamic and continually improving nature of the company.
