Tested Modules

CUBIXEL follows the test-driven development (TDD) methodology when it comes to software. This ensures a higher quality of code for our final product by reduces the number of bugs present in production code by fundamentally changing the process by which the code is written.

Client Involvement

An on-site customer or representative will join our team to help in decisions relating features and to contribute knowledge of the field we are producing software for. The on-site customer should be available to be asked questions at any time during development and is considered an integral part of the team.

CUBIXEL offers high quality and industry standard software development services for partners and external companies. We collaborate and involve the client throughout the project's development to ensure that the final deliverables are within scope and within the discussed time frame.

Quality Assurance

The CUBIXEL company prides itself on its ability to provide quality assurance as a means to ensure a high level of quality in production during the development of products or services.

The CUBIXEL Quality Assurance Manual provides an understanding of what the CUBIXEL company values and what it represents. It details the corporate structure of CUBIXEL and information regarding the responsibilities of each role within our development team. Our QA Manual serves as a reference for how the company's software engineering projects should run, providing details on many aspects of CUBIXEL’s particular approach to project management; we regularly update our QA Manual to reflect the dynamic and continually improving nature of the company.
