What is TutorPoint?

TutorPoint is a desktop based, Java application intended to provide a digital lecture environment for both students and lecturers. It is open to any subject that a tutor would like to teach but with a particular focus on STEM subjects at the college and university level. It provides the tools to enable delivery of lecture content live to an audience with instant feedback from users on topics being covered. Users will be able to create an account as either a student or a lecturer and begin watching or producing content on a subject of their choice.

The aim of TutorPoint is to provide an online platform for students and specialists to share, converse and develop their respective fields. The platform is to be structured in a way as to aid education above any other purpose, incorporating a screen for XML presentations, an interactive whiteboard, and both text and video chat. The goal of TutorPoint is to nurture limitless education in all fields globally, enabling new fields of studies and advancement in developing countries and niche areas of expertise.

Free Registration

Students and tutors can sign up to TutorPoint for free with no upfront costs. Simply enter a unique username and a valid email and password, and you're good to go!

Online Lessons

Choose from a vast selection of certified, curriculum-based subjects and courses, each containing hours of content created by tutors and fellow students within the field.

XML Presentations

Teach students with your own XML-based presentations using text, graphics, images, videos, and audio, interpreted by our own custom XML presentation board.

Interactive Whiteboard

Use additional tools such as our interactive whiteboard to communicate with students in real-time. This can be used by both tutors and students in one-to-one private lessons to collaborate on problems and ideas.
