About Us

CUBIXEL is an eight person software engineering consultancy and development team based in York, United Kingdom. As a team, we provide software design services to clients and large companies, focusing on full stack development and providing true customer values using agile development practices.

Our Mission

We strive to provide companies with the necessary tools to prosper in their respective field. Our business focus is on the design, implementation, and support of research and academic based software for companies willing to push the boundaries of education and research.

Our Vision

Our vision for the company is to advance new areas of education through developing software to encouragin more students into STEM based subjects. We hope this will lead to a more sustainable and eco-friendly environment for future generations by allowing new groundbreaking studies and research.
