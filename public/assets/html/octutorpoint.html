QA Metrics

  • Percentage Iteration Progress Expenditure
    Difference between percentages of expenditure and completion for each week every Thursday.

  • Labour Buffer
    Remaining surplus hours from the budget which can be dynamically assigned to employees for overtime. Tracked weekly every Thursday.

  • Development Cost Difference
    Difference between budgeted programming cost against actual iteration cost. Tracked fortnightly every Thursday.
